HOW TO ADD YOUR AVATAR VIDEO
==============================

1. Record or export your video:
   - Stand against a DARK/BLACK background (easiest to blend)
   - OR use a green screen and remove bg using tools like:
     • remove.bg (free)
     • Unscreen.com (for videos)
     • CapCut "remove background" feature (free, mobile)
   - Crop to show head + shoulders + body (portrait orientation)

2. Name it exactly: avatar.mp4

3. Place it in the /public/ folder:
   manasi-portfolio/public/avatar.mp4

4. Restart dev server — it will appear automatically on the right side.

TIPS:
- Video with transparent background (.webm with alpha) looks BEST
- If using .mp4 with dark bg, the "mix-blend-mode: lighten" CSS
  will make the dark background disappear automatically
- Keep video looped, no audio needed
- Ideal resolution: 720x1080 (portrait) or 1080x1920

For transparent WebM export:
- Use DaVinci Resolve (free) → export as WebM with Alpha
- Or use Adobe After Effects → Media Encoder → WebM
- Change src="/avatar.mp4" to src="/avatar.webm" in Hero.tsx
